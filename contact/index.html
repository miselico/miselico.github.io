About Me

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Fraunhofer Institute for Applied Information Technology FIT (Germany) and based at the RWTH University in Aachen (Germany). The subjects covered in my research are related to

  • Data Mining and Analysis
    • Knowledge Graph Embedding
    • Scalable Hierarchical Clustering
    • Evolutionary Computing (Optimization)
    • Frequent Pattern Mining
  • Knowledge Representation
    • Prototype-Based Ontologies
    • Knowledge Evolution and Matching
    • Ontology Learning and Matching

I have also worked on

  • Multi-Agent Systems
  • Version control in IT education
I have been teaching courses on Service Oriented Architecture, cloud computing, data analysis, and Multi-agent systems.
